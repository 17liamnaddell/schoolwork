# Situation

Dred Scott was living in a free state when he was sold to another slaveholder in a slave state, this court case was argued the question of can a slave be sold to another slaveholder while in a free state.

# Effects

Many abolitionists were furious about how degrading and dehumanizing the dread scott decision was to slaves. It went as far as to make slaves akin to property, which was a opinion that many slaveholders themselves did not have. This helped cause the flame war that led to a real civil war.
